Hailey Reed April 14th, 2025 ★★★★★

During the 2020 pandemic, I stumbled across the movie Ponyo and instantly fell in love with it. Ponyo's visually appealing animation and magical storytelling enchants viewers, often making it hard to look away. Personally, I found its kind, brave, and free-spirited characters to be enchanting and easily lovable. My favorite character is Ponyo because of her feverish curiosity and deep care for Sosuke which seems to add a sense of warmth to the story. Overall, Ponyo is a timeless cinematic masterpiece that I strongly recommend.

Trivia

  • Ponyo's name is a Japanese onomatopoeia for "squishy."
  • The film was inspired by the real-life Japanese town of Tomonoura
  • Soundtrack composer, Joe Hisaishi's, daughter Mai provides vocals on the film's score, adding a unique and personal touch.
