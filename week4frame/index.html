The movie Ponyo follows the story of a curious goldfish princess named Brunhilde (later renamed Ponyo) who encounters and befriends a villager boy named Sosuke. As the daughter of the sea wizard, Fujimoto, and sea goddess, Granmamare, magic has always been a part of Ponyo's life. Thus, when Ponyo magically transforms from a goldfish into a human to be closer to Sosuke, she is ultimately faced with a series of whimsical and dramatic events.

Rating: G

Genre: Anime

Run Time: 1h 43m

Release date: August 14, 2009

Production Company: Studio Ghibli
